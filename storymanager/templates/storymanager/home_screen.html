Welcome To ScrollMaster!

Tired of carrying notes to each of your sessions? Having difficulty keeping track of initiatives and turns? Keep losing that one sheet of notes you really needed to act out a character? Well, ScrollMaster is the software for you! ScrollMaster manages all of the tedious notes that you need for GMing and lets you access them quickly and easily, while still letting your players roll their own dice and manage their own character sheets!

You can create a new story by pressing the "" button. Or you can click an existing story to get started!
